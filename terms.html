While AutoDrop moderates listings and reserves the right to remove suspicious content, we cannot guarantee that every listing is genuine. Users transact entirely at their own risk. Always conduct an in-person inspection before paying any money.

AutoDrop reserves the right — but is not obligated — to remove any Listing that is reported as or suspected to be fraudulent. The removal of a Listing does not constitute an admission of liability on the part of AutoDrop nor does it guarantee that fraud has or has not occurred.

AutoDrop's Safety Recommendations to Buyers
